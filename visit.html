🌿 Welcome to Eka Jiva Reserve

Eka Jiva is a field research and ecological experience site where visitors can explore natural habitats, observe animal behaviour, and engage with real-world ecological systems.

Experiences

  • Guided behaviour observation walks with captive animals
  • Night-time ecological tracking in the forest
  • Introduction to field research methods
  • Seasonal biodiversity surveys
