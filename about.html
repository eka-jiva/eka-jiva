About This Project

What do we do?

Eka Jiva is a field-based research platform focused on understanding animal behaviour in natural environments. We collect structured observations of behaviour across different ecological conditions, integrating field data with scientific reasoning.

Why are we doing it?

Behaviour is one of the most dynamic interfaces between organisms and their environment. Yet, much of it remains underexplored in real-world conditions. This project aims to bridge the gap between controlled research and natural observation by building a dataset grounded in real ecological systems.

Vision

To build a comprehensive, evolving database of animal behaviour that contributes to understanding ecological interactions, behavioural plasticity, and evolutionary processes.

Mission

To systematically document behavioural patterns across environments, generate testable hypotheses, and create an open platform that connects field observations with scientific inquiry.

This platform is dedicated to studying animal behaviour, ecology, and evolutionary patterns through field observations and structured data collection.
