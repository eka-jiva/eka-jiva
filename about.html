About Eka Jiva

Purpose • Vision • Mission

What do we do?

Eka-Jiva is a research-driven ecological platform focused on understanding animal behaviour in natural environments. We integrate structured field observations with ecological and evolutionary reasoning to uncover how behaviour adapts across environments.

Why are we doing it?

Behaviour is the most dynamic interface between organisms and their environment. Yet, it remains underexplored in real-world systems. Eka Jiva bridges the gap between controlled research and natural observation, promoting ecological awareness and sustainable coexistence.

Vision

To build a living system where research, conservation, and human understanding converge. A place where behavioural ecology is not only studied, but experienced.

Mission

To systematically document behaviour across environments, generate hypotheses, and create a platform that connects field observations with scientific inquiry while promoting conservation.
