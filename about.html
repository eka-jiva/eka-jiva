About This Project

What do we do?

Eka-Jiva is a platform focused on understanding animal behaviour in natural environments.We want to use this information to better understand ecology and help conserve them. We want connect human society with the wildlife and help everyone to live together. Along with this, collect structured observations of behaviour across different ecological conditions, integrating field data with scientific reasoning.

Why are we doing it?

Behaviour is one of the most dynamic interfaces between organisms and their environment. Yet, much of it remains underexplored in real-world conditions. This project aims to bridge the gap between controlled research and natural observation by building a dataset grounded in real ecological systems. This helps understand the ecology of the system and allows others to be more aware of using natural resources. Mindful use of these resources would lead to less disruption of the ecosystem and a sustaininable living.

Vision

1) To understand and educate others about wildlife. 2) To build a comprehensive, evolving database of animal behaviour that contributes to understanding ecological interactions, behavioural plasticity, and evolutionary processes. 3) To build place to help people understand wildlife better and conserve it as one.

Mission

1) To study and apply the knowledge on conserving the wildlife, while learning to live alongside with them and not separate them. 2) To systematically document behavioural patterns across environments, generate testable hypotheses, and create an open platform that connects field observations with scientific inquiry.

This platform is dedicated to studying animal behaviour, ecology, and evolutionary patterns through field observations and structured data collection.
